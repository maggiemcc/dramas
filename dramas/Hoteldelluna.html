Hotel del Luna

Hotel del Luna is located in downtown Seoul and run by a woman named Jang Man-Wol. Jang Man-Wol is a beatufiul but greedy woman who due to a mistake she made in her past is stuck at Hotel del Luna as its CEO.

A young hotel manager, Koo Chang-Sung, is a sincere and kind man. Due to something unexpected he ends up working at Hotel del Luna as its new manager. Hotel del Luna customers are ghosts.
